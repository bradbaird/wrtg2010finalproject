BRADBAIRD

Writing 2010 - Dr Gerdes - University of Utah

The intent of my final project may be a little ambiguous, so I have added this section to provide some additional context and hopefully you get a laugh out of it. The game I created can be accessed at the top of the page by clicking the "CLICK HERE" link.

The game was setup like a Jeopardy game, and could be used as such even though the answers may not be what you are thinking. The instructions for how to run the game will be at the top of the screen as you enter questions and proceed through the game.

The intent of the game, and the point of the project was to highlight some of the ludicrous arguments of 2020, poke fun at some of the funny things that humans say, and if you are so inclined, use the game to invite debate into your life with the topics provided.
